What is a Manufacturers Rep:

For Customers, we are the field factory support you get or should get from your vendors. While we are not direct employees of the manufacturer. We provide the same direct access to the manufacturer a direct employee would. You deal with the manufacturers customer service on a day to day basis. For Manufacturers, we provide quality professional representation with no fixed costs. You pay only commissions on items sold: No travel expenses, no payroll, no health insurance, no 401k.

Whether you are distributor or OEM looking for product or a manufacturer looking for representation, please contact us, we look forward to speaking with you.
