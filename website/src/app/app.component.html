What is a Manufacturers Rep?


We are the field factory support you get or should get from your vendors. While we are not direct employees of the manufacturer, we provide the same direct access to the manufacturer a direct employee would. You deal with the manufacturer’s customer service on a day to day basis. You deal with us as you would a field representative (District Manager or Regional Manager type). We call on you to show you new products and applications. We help when something does not go properly and help with custom solutions.

Benefits to the Customer:

· Direct access to the manufacturer
· Local support from the manufacturer

covering several manufacturers

· Field, factory support
